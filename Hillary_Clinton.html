Hillary Clinton


Throughout Clinton's life she has gained a lot of political experience. She has served as the 67th Untied States Secretary of State from 2009 to 2013, the Junior United States Senator representing New York from 2001 to 2009, First Lady of the United States during presidency of husband Bill Clinton from 1993 to 2001, and the First Lady of Arkansas during his governorship from 1979 to 1981 and from 1983 to 1992. If Clinton wins the 2016 presidential election she will become the first ever woman to be president of the United States of America.
